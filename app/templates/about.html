About the System

Designed for doctors who need control, speed, and patient trust

This website stack combines clinical presentation pages, operational workflows, and patient communication tools in one secure Python backend.

What the doctor can do

  • Publish and edit website pages with a built-in CMS.
  • Upload website files, documents, and media assets from admin dashboard.
  • Track appointment requests with status controls.
  • Respond to support tickets from patients with full conversation history.
  • Offer AI-based website guidance while avoiding diagnosis claims.

Compliance-minded defaults

The platform stores structured logs for appointment and support interactions. For production, configure stronger auth policies, audit integrations, and encryption.
